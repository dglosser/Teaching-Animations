What the letters mean. CPR = corrosion penetration rate (how deep the surface eats in per year). K = 87.6, a unit constant that makes the answer come out in mm/yr when W is in mg, ρ in g/cm³, A in cm², t in hours. A rate under ~0.5 mm/yr is fine for most structures; above ~2 mm/yr the part won't last.
