Flaws concentrate stress

σ_max = K_t · σ₀ — a sharp feature multiplies the stress right at its tip · ENGR 170

The average stress on a part tells you almost nothing about where it breaks. A hole, notch, or sharp corner makes the local stress much higher at the feature. Sharpen the notch and watch K_t — and the peak stress — climb. This is why designers round every corner.
