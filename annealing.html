Annealing: undoing cold work

Heat a cold-worked metal and it resets in three stages · ENGR 170

Cold work leaves a metal hard, brittle, and full of tangled dislocations. Raise the annealing temperature and three processes run in sequence — recovery → recrystallization → grain growth — trading strength back for ductility. Drag the temperature and watch the microstructure and properties change.
