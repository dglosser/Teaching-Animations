Two ways an atom moves

Vacancy vs. interstitial diffusion · ENGR 170

Both mechanisms are just atoms hopping — but the substitutional atom must wait for a neighboring vacancy, while the small interstitial squeezes through the gaps freely. Watch the hop counters diverge.
