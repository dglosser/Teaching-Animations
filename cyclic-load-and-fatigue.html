Cyclic load & fatigue life

σ_m, σ_a, Δσ, R from σ_max and σ_min — and what they do to life · ENGR 170

Any repeating load is described by just two numbers (σ_max and σ_min); everything else follows. Drag them and watch the wave, the four parameters, and the estimated fatigue life. The lesson: amplitude drives fatigue — lowering σ_a is the surest way to extend life, and for steel there's a threshold below which life is effectively infinite.
