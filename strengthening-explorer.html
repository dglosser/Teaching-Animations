Three ways to strengthen a metal

Grain refinement · solid solution · cold work — all obstruct dislocations · ENGR 170

Each lever adds a different obstacle to dislocation motion, so each adds to the yield strength. But watch ductility: two of the three cost it — only grain refinement raises strength without wrecking ductility.
