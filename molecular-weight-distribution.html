Molecular weight is a distribution

Mn and Mw are two ways of averaging the same set of chains · ENGR 170

Real polymers are a mixture of chain lengths. The number-average weights every chain equally; the weight-average weights each chain by its own mass — so it always sits toward the long-chain tail.
