The two formulas. Along: Ec = Vf·Ef + Vm·Em — a weighted average, so the stiff fibers dominate. Across: 1/Ec = Vf/Ef + Vm/Em — the soft matrix sits in the load path in series and drags stiffness down. Here Vf is the fiber fraction and Vm = 1 − Vf is the matrix fraction.
